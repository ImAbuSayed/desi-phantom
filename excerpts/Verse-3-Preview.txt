Dhoke de saaye ch main fast drive karda,
Teri mithi gallan ch dhoke pure lies farda,
Seeshe ton bahaar main cold breeze sahnda,
Tere vaadeyan ton azaad free bird ban da.
Tyeran di cheekh ch main lost track mannda,
Ajj vi main mud ke oh true face bhalda,
Main jhalleyan wangu tera blind wait karda,
Tainu bhullan di munda hard try karda.
