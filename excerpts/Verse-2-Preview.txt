Kaali is sadak te main late night bhajjda,
Lohan da aswaar ajj hard beat vajda,
Tere naina da teer ajj sharp strike karda,
Sadkan di tapash ch main hot fire farda.
Bulla di chuppi te kudiye bright light dharda,
Zehar jeha roop tera slow poison karda,
Tu sohne khyalan ch perfect art lagdi,
Main dewana aashiq ni mad rules pakda.
